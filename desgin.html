Frequently Asked Questions

What devices are compatible with the app?
Our app is compatible with iOS devices running iOS 13+ (iPhone 6s or newer) and Android devices with ARCore support (most devices from 2017 onward). For the best experience, we recommend using devices with newer processors and more RAM.
How accurate are the measurements?
Our AR measurement technology is accurate to within 1-2% when used correctly. For best results, ensure good lighting and move your device slowly when scanning your room. We recommend double-checking critical measurements before making purchase decisions.
Is my data and privacy protected?
Absolutely. We take privacy seriously. Your room scans are stored locally on your device unless you choose to back them up to our secure cloud service. We never share your personal data or room layouts with third parties without your explicit permission.
Can I use the app without internet access?
Basic AR functionality works offline once the app is installed. However, browsing new furniture items, making purchases, and syncing across devices requires an internet connection. We recommend downloading your favorite items beforehand if you know you'll be without internet.
